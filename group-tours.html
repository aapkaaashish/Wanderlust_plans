Frequently Asked Questions

Are flights included in the price? ▼

For domestic tours (Ladakh, Kerala), flights/trains to the starting point are NOT included. For international tours (Bali, Vietnam), round-trip flights from major Indian cities ARE included unless specified otherwise.

Can I join solo? ▼

Absolutely! Over 60% of our travelers join solo. We have a roommate matching system, or you can pay a single supplement for a private room.

What is the cancellation policy? ▼

Free cancellation up to 30 days before departure. 50% refund between 30-15 days. No refund within 15 days as we pre-book non-refundable services.
